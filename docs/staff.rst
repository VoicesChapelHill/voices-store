Staff View of the site
======================

Staff are those who need to change the products offered, find out
what has been sold, etc etc.

Staff must have an account on the site (register as usual), then are
granted additional staff privileges by someone, and after that,
they have access to more things on the site.

Staff page
----------

The staff page is the main dashboard/page for staff to start
from.

Staff get to the staff page using a link under "My Account" that
will only appear when a staff member is logged in.
